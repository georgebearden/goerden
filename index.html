What weve done

With over a decade of combined experience, we have been leading and developing front-end/back-end software for companies including Amazon, The Wright State Research Institute, InfoSciTex, Ball Aerospace and the Department of Defense. Past achievements include

  • leading development of the authentication libraries used by several desktop teams at Amazon
  • developing key components of Amazon's Cloud Drive for Windows and Cloud Drive for Mac desktop applications
  • the creation of an autonomy test bed that has seen tremendous growth and use within the Department of Defense
  • assisting the NASA Ames Research Center with initial studies for their efforts of binging unmanned aircraft systems into the national airspace system (UAS in the NAS)
  • developing geo-spatial search engine technologies
  • creating several C++, C#, and Java libraries (including 2D/3D mapping toolkits, real-time video encoding/decoding, and high-fidelity simulation modules for testing present and future airpower capabilities) that are currently in use by the Department of Defense
  • co-authored several research publications
  • presented research and software findings at a number of conferences
