What we've done

Before Erden, our developers accrued over a decade of combined experience leading and developing solutions for Fortune 500, aerospace and defense companies. Since Erden’s founding, we have been designing, developing, and deploying front-end and back-end software solutions, as well as playing a critical role in several commercial product lines. Individual past achievements include:

  • Led development of authentication libraries for cloud-based services in Xamarin and .Net
  • Led development of restful web-services using NodeJS deployed to Amazon Web Services
  • Led development of web-based, interactive tools using ActionScript, Flash and Flex
  • Created an autonomy test bed currently in widespread use throughout the DOD
  • Assisted the NASA Ames Research Center with initial studies for their efforts in bringing unmanned aircraft systems into the national airspace system
  • Developed geo-spatial search engine technologies using several Google technologies (Google Search Appliance, Google Maps, Google Web Toolkit
  • Created several C++, C#, and Java libraries currently used by the DOD (including 2D/3D mapping toolkits, real-time video codecs, and high-fidelity simulation modules for testing present and future airpower capabilities)
We have a passion for software engineering, as evidenced by several co-authored research publications and professional conference presentations.
