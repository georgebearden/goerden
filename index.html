Who we are

Erden LLC is a team of thinkers and dreamers. We are contemplative during analysis and pragmatic during implementation. Our solutions are lean, and solid to ensure you get exactly what you need now and in the future.

What we've done

With over a decade of combined experience, we have been leading and developing front-end and back-end software for companies including Amazon, The Wright State Research Institute, InfoSciTex, Ball Aerospace and the Department of Defense. Our past achievements include

  • leading development of the authentication libraries used by several desktop teams at Amazon
  • developing key components of Amazon's Cloud Drive for Windows and Cloud Drive for Mac desktop applications
  • the creation of an autonomy test bed that has seen sizeable growth and use throughout the Department of Defense
  • assisting the NASA Ames Research Center with initial studies for their efforts of bringing unmanned aircraft systems into the national airspace system
  • developing geo-spatial search engine technologies
  • creating several C++, C#, and Java libraries (including 2D/3D mapping toolkits, real-time video codecs, and high-fidelity simulation modules for testing present and future airpower capabilities)
We have a passion for software engineering, as evidenced by several co-authored research publications and professional conference presentations.
